## What is the Testing Phase?

This phase is **crucial and essential** to validate all functionalities, commands, and critical flows of our blockchain before the official launch. During this period, we will carry out:

- **Exhaustive testing rounds**: We will evaluate the stability, security, and performance of the blockchain
- **Load testing**: We will verify the system's capacity under different conditions
- **Critical process validation**: We will practice and perfect procedures that will only be executed once on mainnet
- **Optimizations**: We will adjust and improve the system based on the results obtained

## Testing Objectives

Our main objective is to ensure that Infinite Improbability Drive is completely prepared for its public launch, meeting the highest standards of:

- **Security**: Data and transaction protection
- **Performance**: Efficiency and processing speed
- **Scalability**: Growth and adaptation capacity
- **Reliability**: System stability and availability

## Testing Round Characteristics

### Cyclical Testing

A key characteristic is that **it will not be a single testing round**, but rather we will perform **cyclical rounds**:

- We will perform different testing rounds cyclically
- **The coordination of when we will perform the rounds and when we will restart the chains** will be done through our **Telegram channel in the testing subsection**
- This will allow us to collect metrics from each round, compare them, and analyze them
- Each round will bring us closer to a perfect and standardized launch process

> ðŸ“± **Important**: While this blog contains the **instructions and complete flow** of what we will do, **real-time coordination** of testing rounds will be done through our **Telegram channel**. We recommend joining to stay up to date with announcements of new rounds and chain restarts.

### Accelerated Unlocking Period

- On mainnet, the period defined for gradual token unlocking is **42 years**
- During testing rounds, this period will last only **1 week**
- This will allow us to experience the unlocking and release process at an accelerated pace
- We will be able to validate various behaviors of the economic system in a compressed time

## How to Participate?

Everyone has the option to support the project in two ways:

1. **Maintain a validator node**
2. **Maintain an RPC node**

Each of these node types has essentially the same node functionality, but with different purposes:

- **Validator Node**: Responsible for validating transactions and keeping the network operational
- **RPC Node**: Responsible for providing or enabling services or endpoints for use by applications or users

**Tokens for Participants**

All those who participate in the chain and set up a node (whether validator or RPC) will receive [42] tokens. Each participant will have the free decision of what to do with them:

- Create their own validator
- Delegate them to other validators
- Use them for various tests (such as when we need to test transfers or transactions)

### Phase 4: Iterative Testing

After the first round, we will continue with cyclical rounds that will include:

- **Chain Launch restarts**: We will launch the chain again and again on the different chains we have (mainnet, testnet, and creative) at different times
- **Specific tests**: We will perform specific tests of different elements or components over time
- **Token movement**: Validation of transfers and economic operations
- **DAO voting**: Governance system testing
- **Scenario simulation**: Tests of different conditions and extreme cases
- **Functionality validation**: Tests of specific chain features

All these activities will be coordinated in real time through our **Telegram channel**. Each round will allow us to collect metrics, compare results, and perfect the system.
